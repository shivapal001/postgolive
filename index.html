How It Works

Simple 3-step process to start collaborating

1

Choose a Plan & Sign Up

Select your plan — Free, Starter, or Pro. Create your account as a Founder or Creator and complete your profile.

2

Founder Sends Collaboration Request

Browse creator profiles and send a collaboration request with your proposal and requirements.

3

Connect on WhatsApp

Once your request is sent, creator's WhatsApp is revealed (based on your plan) and you can start collaborating instantly.

FAQ

Your Questions
Answered

How does WhatsApp access work?
Free plan: You send a request, creator accepts, then WhatsApp is revealed. Starter plan: WhatsApp shown when you send the request (20/month). Pro plan: WhatsApp always visible, unlimited requests.
Is payment secure?
Yes! We use Razorpay — India's most trusted payment gateway. All transactions are encrypted and secure. We accept UPI, cards, net banking, and wallets.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes! Upgrade anytime from your dashboard. The new plan activates immediately after successful payment.
How do I become a featured creator?
Creators on the Pro plan automatically get the Featured badge and top search placement, making them more visible to founders.
