Our Story
From a Class 12 classroom to a platform for India's creators.

PostHire started with a simple frustration: creators were spending more time hunting for brand deals than creating content, and startups were wasting weeks trying to find the right creator for their campaigns.

Shiva Pal, a 16-year-old from Punjab, saw this gap while still studying for his Class 12 exams. Instead of waiting to graduate, he started building a solution — a platform that would make collaboration between creators and brands structured, fast, and honest.

He was joined by Hitesh Kaushik, who brought deep expertise in AI, machine learning and product architecture, and Nawazish Ashraf, who shaped the creator ecosystem strategy and community operations. Together, they built PostHire into what it is today — India's emerging home for creator-brand collaboration.

Why we exist

India's creator economy is one of the fastest-growing in the world, but most collaboration still happens through cold DMs, unreliable middlemen, or expensive agencies. PostHire is the alternative — a marketplace built directly for founders, small brands, and digital creators who deserve better infrastructure.
