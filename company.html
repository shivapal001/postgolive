Your Data

Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. Here's exactly what we collect, why, and how we protect it.

Last updated: April 2026

What We Collect
Basic account data: Your name, email address, and WhatsApp number (optional) when you sign up. This is used to create and identify your account.
Profile information: Bio, niche, audience size, social handles, and other details you choose to add to your profile. This is visible to other users.
Usage data: How you use PostHire — pages visited, features used, and collaboration activity. This helps us improve the platform.
How We Protect It
All data is stored securely on Firebase (Google Cloud infrastructure) with encryption at rest and in transit.
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party, ever.
WhatsApp numbers are only shared between a founder and creator when a collaboration request is accepted by both parties.

You can update or correct your personal information from your profile settings at any time.
